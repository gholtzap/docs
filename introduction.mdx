# Welcome to Solisa Calendar

`solisa-calendar` is a versatile calendar library designed to help you integrate scheduling and event management experiences into your application. With support for multiple calendar views and extensive customization options, Solisa Calendar empowers you to create the perfect calendar solution for your project.
